Chapati Egg and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Chapati
  • Two eggs whisked
  • Shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Heat a greased pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook both sides of the chapati until browned, then set aside.
  3. Pour whisked eggs into the pan, cooking and browning both sides.
  4. Sprinkle your chosen shredded cheese over the eggs and let it melt.
  5. Return the chapati to the pan.
  6. Heat and flip once more.
  7. Fold and enjoy
